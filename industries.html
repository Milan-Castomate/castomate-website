Industries We Serve

Castomate Technologies provides specialized foundry solutions to a diverse range of industries. Our products are engineered to meet the specific challenges and quality requirements of each sector, ensuring optimal performance and reliability.

[icon: Steel Mill]

Steel Casting

High-performance consumables for various steel casting applications, including carbon steel, alloy steel, and stainless steel. Our products help improve melt quality, reduce defects, and enhance overall efficiency.

[icon: Gear]

SG Iron (Ductile Iron) Casting

Specialized solutions for SG iron production, focusing on achieving desired microstructures, mechanical properties, and minimizing casting defects. Includes filters, inoculants, and sleeves.

[icon: Aluminum Ingot]

Aluminum Casting

Products designed for aluminum foundries, including degassing agents, fluxes, filters, and coatings to ensure high-quality, defect-free aluminum castings for automotive, aerospace, and other applications.

[icon: Factory]

Gray Iron Casting

A comprehensive range of products to support gray iron casting, from melt treatment to molding and finishing, enhancing productivity and casting integrity.

[icon: Heavy Machinery]

Heavy Engineering & Machinery

Robust solutions for foundries producing large and complex castings for heavy machinery, power generation, and infrastructure projects.

[icon: Car]

Automotive Components

Precision products for automotive foundries manufacturing engine blocks, cylinder heads, transmission parts, and other critical components demanding tight tolerances and high performance.
